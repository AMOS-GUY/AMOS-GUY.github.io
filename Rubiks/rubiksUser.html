How to Use

  1. Hold your physical Rubik's Cube with the WHITE face on top and GREEN face facing you
  2. Click on each sticker in the virtual cube below to match your physical cube's colors
  3. Use the color palette to select colors
  4. Click "Validate Cube" to check for errors
  5. Click "Solve" to get step-by-step instructions
